About

2PD Generator is a simple tool that creates a printable wheel for two-point discrimination testing. Download one of our presets, or enter 8 distances (0-17 mm) and generate your own!

This project was created by Anthony Shadid and Keyvon Rashidi. We created the 3D model and built this website to make these tools more accessible, customizable, and easy to use.

Intended Use

Designed for education, research prototyping, and demonstration. This tool has not been evaluated or cleared as a medical device. For clinical use, follow your institution’s policies.

Quick Start

• Enter 8 distances between 0-17 millimeters (e.g., 2, 3, 4, 5, 8, 12, 14, 16)
• Tip: distances should progressively increase for easy clinical use
• Click “Generate Custom STL”
• Download the file
• 3D-print the wheel (see printing guide)

Printing Guide

• Material: PLA or PETG
• Layer height: 0.16–0.20 mm (smaller tips benefit from finer layers)
• Walls: 2–3 perimeters
• Infill: 20–40%
• Orientation: print flat
• Check tip integrity, lightly deburr tips if needed

Cleaning & Handling

• Wipe with 70% isopropyl alcohol before and after demonstration.
• Avoid harsh sterilization/heat; printed plastics can deform.
• Inspect tips regularly. Discontinue use if damaged.

Model Parameters

• Wheel radius: 35 mm
• Hub radius: 14 mm
• Thickness: 3 mm
• Tip radius: 0.7 mm
• Tip length: 3 mm
• Facets: 48

Validation & Accuracy

Distances are modeled directly from your inputs. Final printed distances may vary depending on printer calibration and settings. Verify with calipers if accuracy is important.

FAQ

What units are used?
Millimeters (mm). STL is unitless, but we follow mm convention.

Privacy

No personal data is collected. Files are generated and downloaded directly by the user.
