2PD Generator

Version 1.0

About

2PD Generator is a simple tool that creates a printable wheel for two-point discrimination testing. Enter up to 8 distances (mm) and the site generates an STL you can download and print.

This project was created by Anthony Shadid and Keyvon Rashidi. We created the 3D model and built this website to make these tools more accessible, customizable, and easy to use.

Intended Use

Designed for education, research prototyping, and demonstration. This tool has not been evaluated or cleared as a medical device. For clinical use, follow your institution’s policies.

Quick Start

• Enter distances in millimeters (e.g., 2, 3, 4, 5, 8, 12, 14, 16)
• TIP: distances >17 are too far apart and will not print well
• Click “Generate STL”
• Download the file
• Print the wheel

Printing Guide

Material: PLA or PETG
Layer height: 0.16–0.20 mm
Walls: 2–3 perimeters
Infill: 20–40%
Orientation: print flat
Lightly deburr tips if needed

Model Parameters

Wheel radius: 35 mm
Hub radius: 14 mm
Thickness: 3 mm
Tip radius: 0.7 mm
Tip length: 3 mm
Facets: 48

Validation & Accuracy

Distances are modeled directly from your inputs. Final printed distances may vary depending on printer calibration and settings. Verify with calipers if accuracy is important.

FAQ

What units are used?
Millimeters (mm). STL files follow mm convention.

Privacy

No personal data is collected. Files are generated and downloaded directly by the user.
